Hi, I'm Ryan

Currently studying Computer Science at New York University

Interested in data , design and making boring things interesting

Baptised as a geek by the videogames of my childhood, I've always had an interest in computers. Playing games like Zelda and Halo introduced me to the idea of fictional yet impossibly, inhabitable virtual worlds. Jailbreaking my Ipod and creating custom desktop widgets with Rainmeter was the next stop on my digital journey. Naturally as a middle schooler I was mostly interested in animating icons and displaying a resource monitor to make me seem like a "hacker". Despite very little programming experience I realized early on that I wanted to make tools that made the ordinary extraordinary and that I might need some magic. Upon further research I concluded that the closest thing to magic I could find was programming. Evinced by the military term PFM, the result of beautiful code is often interpreted as Pure F%&*@#g Magic. In my work I hope to harness this power to create a experience that is weightless, easy and most importantly, wonderful. I hope you enjoy my site and work as much as I do. Thanks for reading.
